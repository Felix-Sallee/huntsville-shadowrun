## Setting Notes

Huntsville, 2058. The UCAS defense corridor runs through here — Ares Macrotechnology subcontractors, old NASA ghosts rebranded as Aztechnology research partners, and the remnants of Southern gentility rotting under a neon sky. The money is old, the corps are newer, and the shadows are deep.
