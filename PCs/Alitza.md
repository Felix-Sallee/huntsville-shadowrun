# ALITZA

**Player:** Jennifer
**Archetype:** Decker / Rigger
**Metatype:** Human (Female)
**Age:** 28
**Appearance:** Black hair, short-cropped. Dark brown eyes. 5'4", lean build. Usually in a worn Ares corp jacket she refuses to throw out.

---

## Background

Former Ares Macrotechnology systems analyst assigned to the Redstone Arsenal contract corridor. Discovered her decking work was being laundered through a shell subsidiary and sold to Aztechnology — with her name on the access logs. She burned her SIN before the audit closed, grabbed what she could carry, and walked into the Huntsville shadows.

Now she runs tech. Drones watch what she can't, the deck opens what's locked, and the van gets everybody out. She doesn't trust corporations, she's fine with that, and she's very good at her job.

## Notes

- When **jumped into a vehicle or drone**, VCR 2 grants +2 dice to vehicle control tests and full sensory integration with the vehicle's sensor suite.
- **Rigging simultaneously** across multiple drones uses the Reaction stat and initiative passes — manage drone autonomy (Autopilot ratings) for drones not in direct control.
- **Deck programs** are loaded to Active Memory (600 Mp capacity). Swap programs from Storage Memory between runs as needed.
- **Smartlink II** applies only when using the Ares Predator II with SmartGun link installed. +2 dice added to Pistols pool.
- **Layered armor** — if total exceeds Body, apply encumbrance penalties per SR3E combat chapter.
- Cyberware costs marked as estimates; cross-check against SR3E Cyberware table for final values. Program costs similarly estimated.
